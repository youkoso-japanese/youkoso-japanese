YOUKOSO JAPANESE LANGUAGE INSTITUTE WEBSITE
==========================================

Files:
- index.html
- courses.html
- gallery.html
- papers.html
- registration.html
- admin.html
- assets/css/style.css
- assets/js/main.js
- assets/img/*
- assets/video/japanese-theme-bg.mp4
- assets/papers/*

How to use:
1. Extract the ZIP.
2. Open index.html in your browser.
3. Replace logo-placeholder.svg with your real logo if needed.
4. Replace images in assets/img/ and PDFs in assets/papers/ with your real files.

Notes:
- Dark/light mode works from the top-right button.
- Language switcher supports English, Sinhala, and Japanese.
- Forms save data locally in the browser for the demo admin panel.
- WhatsApp button is connected to the Raddolugama office number.
